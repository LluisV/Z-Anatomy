        Fibrous joints of free upper limb:
            -Radio-ulnar syndesmoses

        Synovial joints of free upper limb:

                -Humeroradial joint
                -Proximal radio-ulnar joint

            -Radiocarpal joint
            -Joints of hand:
                -Intercarpal joints

                -Intermetacarpal joints
                -Metacarpophalangeal joints
                -Interphalangeal joints of hand
