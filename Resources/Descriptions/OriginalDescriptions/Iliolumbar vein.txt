

ILIOLUMBAR VEIN


The iliolumbar vein is the vena comitans of the iliolumbar artery.
The obturator nerve crosses superficial to it.A single vein is found more commonly than a double vein.It drains vertebral segments four and five.It is closely related to the ascending lumbar vein.
